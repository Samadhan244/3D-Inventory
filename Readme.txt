Use W/A/S/D/Q/E to move player/camera
Use LMB to drag items and also use MouseScroll while dragging item and move it to the chest to put it inside.
Pressing "I" opens and closes inventory. (You can check inventoy Weight, TakenItemsCount/MaxItemsCount)
Use LMB-s ButtonUp to drop items from inventory. (The inventory is automatically sorted)
